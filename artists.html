Mitski



Mitsuki Frances Laycock, known professionally as Mitski, was born on September 27, 1990 in the Mie prefecture of Japan. Mitski was eighteen when she wrote her first song. Mitski's family settled in Turkey for some of the time she was in high school though she did not complete her senior year, explaining in an interview that “I realized I had all the credits needed to graduate... I went to the principal’s office and just asked for my degree. So I graduated early.” When she was around 16 and living in Japan, she was working with a pop idol manager who gave her songs geared towards creating a cute, J-pop image.

Joji

George Kusunoki Miller(born 18 September 1992), better known by his stage name Joji and formerly by his online aliases Filthy Frank and Pink Guy, is a Japanese singer-songwriter, record producer, author, comedian, and a former Internet personality and YouTuber. Aside from the comedic and often rap-based music he created under the Pink Guy alias, Miller also created more serious and traditional music under another stage name, Joji, which became his primary focus in late 2017. Speaking on his transition from his YouTube career to his music career as Joji, Miller said to Billboard "now I get to do stuff that I want to hear." In the article by Billboard, he specified that 'Joji' isn't a character like Filthy Frank and Pink Guy. "I guess that's the difference," he continues. "Joji's just me."

Jack Stauber

Jack Stauber is an American independent singer and songwriter from Erie, Pennsylvania, born on April 6th, 1996, and is 25 years old. Jack Stauber's current music style is known for being very strange and complex, but it is mainly a indie pop-synth like, electronic, alternative, and avant-garde style with a unique blend of different other genres in it, like rock, lo-fi, and folk. Jack is also known for having a very strange and stretchy singing voice.
